   f. Security implications — does this change affect the security posture
3. Assign an overall risk level using the ITIL risk matrix:
   - Likelihood of failure (1-5) x Impact of failure (1-5) = Risk score
4. Identify specific risk factors and mitigation strategies for each.
5. Recommend approval path: standard approval, CAB review, or emergency CAB.
