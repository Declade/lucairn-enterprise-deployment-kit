# Customer DPA AI-Tools Clause

Negotiate the AI-tools clause before contract close. Do not handle it as an exception later.

## Expected Shapes

1. No third-party AI tools on Customer Data.

   Response: hand-code or use local-only models for this customer.

2. AI tools allowed through customer-controlled gateway.

   Response: the customer's self-hosted Lucairn deployment is the gateway.

3. AI tools allowed with zero-retention.

   Response: use an enterprise zero-retention plan such as Anthropic Enterprise or OpenAI zero retention when contractually sufficient.

## Requirement

Get the clause in writing before close. Store it with the customer contract record and mirror the operating constraint into the customer project folder.
